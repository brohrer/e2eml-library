If you want to study machine learning, but don't have the luxury of attending university full-time, you're in luck. There is a wonderfully rich collection of courses, posts, videos, notebooks, and tutorials online. There is so much, in fact, that it can be overwhelming. I wanted to put together this guide as a starting place, a first foothold for anyone who wants to jump in.

To pull together these recommendations, I sent out a call for help on LinkedIn and Twitter. The replies there show not just how much is out there, but how passionate people are about the teachers that helped a concept click for them.
